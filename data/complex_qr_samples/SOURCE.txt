Source: BoofCV QR Code benchmark dataset
Dataset URL: https://boofcv.org/notwiki/regression/fiducial/qrcodes_v3.zip
Reference page: https://boofcv.org/index.php?title=Performance:QrCode

Selected categories:
- blurred: motion/defocus blur
- brightness: low/high brightness variation
- bright_spots: local bright spots
- damaged: damaged or partially degraded QR codes
- glare: reflective glare
- perspective: tilted perspective deformation
- rotations: rotated QR codes
- shadows: shadow interference
- curved: curved surface QR codes
- lots: multiple QR codes in one image
- nominal: normal reference cases

These images are used as complex-scene test samples for the course design.
